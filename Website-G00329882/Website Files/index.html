The Greenway Hostel

THe Greenway Hostel is situated in Newport Co.Mayo, right in the heart of the Great Western Greenway and along the famous Wild Atlantic Way.

The Greenway Hostel is the ideal place to start your exploration of West Mayo. Many outdoor activities are easy to find and enjoy. The Greenway is the longest off-road walking and cycling trail in Ireland and extends 42km from Westport to Achill Island. It is perfect for families and those who wish to lose themselves in the natural beauty of Mayo's countryside.

We are also perfectly suited for advetureous hillwalkers with the famous Bangor Trail and the Nephin Beg range located close by.
