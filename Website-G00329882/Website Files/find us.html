Find us

How to find Newport

Located on the Main Street of Newport town. The hostel is between Icones ice cream parlour and Chambers newsagents. The local bus stops directly in front of the hostel entrance.

By Car

If you are traveling from from Westport take the N57 road in the direction of Achill Island (10 km). Traveling from Castlebar take the R311 road heading west.

By Bus

Newport is serviced by the bus route 440 from Westport and operates daily with the bus stop directly in front of the hostel entrance.On Sundays route 440 does not operate however Expressway route 52 provides an evening journey each way to/from Westport and Galway

By Train

To reach Newport by train take the Heuston to Westport train from Heuston Station in Dublin city (daily service).From Westport take the N57 road to Newport (10 km) which follows along the Greenway.
